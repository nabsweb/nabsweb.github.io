About this website
My name is Victor and I am a pre-teen living in Romania. I made this webpage to serve the purpose of being my web-journal. So welcome! I will document my findings here, along with my adventures that I will be encountering along the way. I like mopeds, especially the ones made by Piaggio (Ciao, Bravo, Si, Boxer, etc..). I also tinker with electronics and computer related stuff. I sometimes draw aswell. Last thing: please sign my guestbook. Thanks for reading and I hope you enjoy surfing this webpage!

Note to my adult readers
Please monitor your children when they are surfing the web. There are alot of creeps on the internet and it hurts me to see kids fall under the wrong hands. Remind them to never, never give their personal information (address, phone number and such). Happy surfing!
