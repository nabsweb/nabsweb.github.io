About this website
 My name is Victor and I am a pre-teen living in Romania. I made this webpage to serve the purpose of being my web-journal. So welcome! I will document my findings here, along with my adventures that I will be encountering along the way. I really like tinkering with computers and my dream is to start my own recycled computer shop (like Free Geek)!  I also sometimes draw. Last thing: please sign my guestbook. Thanks for reading and I hope you enjoy surfing this webpage!

Note to adult readers
 Please monitor your children when they are surfing the web. There are alot of creeps on the internet and it hurts me to see kids fall under the wrong hands. Remind them to never, never give their personal information (address, phone number and such). Happy surfing!
